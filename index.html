Research

  • Focus: LLM data synthesis, Efficient AIGC

My long-term research interest lies in how intelligence can grow and scale while remaining precisely calibrated to the complexity of the real world.

Specifically, I am studying how to use agents to synthesize data, improve LLM performance in science-related domains, and build benchmarks that science needs. As human-generated data is compressed into models, synthetic data is key for unbounded growth. Simultaneously, high-quality benchmarks are essential to calibrate model intelligence and serve as fine-grained reward signals for reinforcement learning.
