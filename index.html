Research

My long-term research vision is to bridge the gap between scalable intelligence and physical reality.

Currently, I focus on LLM Data Synthesis and Efficient AIGC. I investigate how to leverage agents for high-quality data synthesis to scale post-training, particularly in scientific domains. Simultaneously, I am building rigorous benchmarks to align model capabilities with complex real-world dynamics, treating evaluation as the ultimate reward signal for reinforcement learning.
